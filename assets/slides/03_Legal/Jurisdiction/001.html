Finally, a few words on jurisdiction.

You will no doubt be told by Republia that this tribunal does not have jurisdiction to entertain AeroSonic's claim because it fails to meet the definition of an investor under Article 72 of the EPA. That position is without merit.

It is undisputed that AeroSonic is incorporated in Singapore, that it's majority owned and controled by a Singaporean entity, and that majority of its board is appointed by Sing-A-Port, in proportion to its shareholding.
